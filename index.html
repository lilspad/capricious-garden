WE LOVE THE FUSS.

At Capricious we like our plants a little dramatic. Didn't get watered today? Wilt. A bit of a draft? Curl. All these theatrics are actually clear and effective communication that helps us understand their needs and respond accordingly. That's our secret to a centre full of healthy, happy babies. And don't forget - when they act up on their every need and fancy, they put on a wondrous show when all is right. Ah, what a rewarding hobby.

We want to share our love and care for gardening, so as well as offering tips and guidance to anyone passing by, we hold regular workshops both for amateers and seasoned planters!
