WE LOVE THE FUSS.

At Capricious we like our plants a little dramatic. Didn't get watered today? Wilt. A bit of a draft? Curl. All these theatrics are actually clear and effective communication that helps us understand their needs and respond accordingly. That's our secret to a centre full of healthy, happy babies.

We want to share our love and care for plants, so as well as offering tips and guidance to anyone passing by, we hold regular classes both for amateurs and seasoned planters!

Pre-loved plants.

We opened Capricious Plant centre in 2003 with plants rescued from neglect. We could not stand aside seeing retailers treating plants like pieces of furniture.
All plants take some time adjusting to new enviroments, and can get a little anxious when you take them home. That is why we make sure plants at Capricious are free from pests and diseases, are in perfect health, and that much more likely to thrive in their new home!
